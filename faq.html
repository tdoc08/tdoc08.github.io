General & What is Permanent Jewelry

Permanent jewelry is clasp-free jewelry that's custom-fitted and welded directly onto your wrist, ankle, neck, or finger using a small, painless spark welder. There's no clasp or fastener — the chain forms a seamless loop around your body part of choice.

Because there's no clasp, you never take it off. It stays on through showers, swimming, sleep, and everyday life. It truly becomes a part of you.

Not at all! The welding process uses a small spark to fuse the end links of your chain together. It takes less than a second and is completely painless. Many clients describe it as a tiny flash of light — nothing more. We protect your skin with a small shield during the weld.

With proper care, permanent jewelry can last for years. Our 14k gold filled and sterling silver chains are designed to withstand daily wear, water exposure, and activity. Many of our clients have worn their pieces for a year or more without any issues.

Solid gold pieces will last indefinitely. The quality of your chain directly affects its longevity — that's why we only carry high-quality materials.

Yes! The small amount of metal in permanent jewelry chains typically does not trigger airport metal detectors or body scanners. Thousands of our clients travel regularly with their permanent jewelry without any issues.

If you're ever concerned, simply mention to the TSA agent that you have a small welded jewelry piece — they are familiar with permanent jewelry.

Yes — permanent doesn't mean impossible to remove. It just means there's no clasp to open. If you ever need to remove your jewelry (for surgery, MRI, personal preference), a simple pair of small scissors or nail clippers can snip the chain. You can always come back to get it re-welded!

Gold filled jewelry has a thick layer of real 14k gold that is mechanically bonded to a base metal core. This is different from gold-plated jewelry, which has only a very thin coating that can wear off quickly.

Gold filled jewelry is durable, tarnish-resistant, and safe for people with sensitive skin. It maintains its appearance for years with normal wear. It's an excellent choice that offers the look and feel of gold at a more accessible price point than solid gold.

The price of gold changes daily on commodity markets. Rather than locking in a fixed price that might not reflect the actual gold value, we price solid gold chains at the current market rate. This ensures you're paying a fair price and getting genuine solid gold — not an inflated markup.

Care & Maintenance

Yes! Permanent jewelry is designed to be worn 24/7, including in the shower, pool, ocean, and hot tub. This is one of its greatest advantages — no need to take it off before getting wet.

For the longest lasting appearance, we do suggest rinsing off any chlorine or salt water after swimming and patting dry gently.

High-quality 14k gold filled jewelry is very tarnish-resistant. Sterling silver may develop a patina over time with constant wear, which is natural. If you notice any tarnishing on sterling silver, a gentle jewelry polishing cloth will restore the shine.

We recommend avoiding prolonged exposure to chlorine, harsh chemicals, and perfumes directly on the chain.

Our chains are very durable and rarely break during normal wear. However, if it does break, don't worry — save the pieces and come see us. We can re-weld your chain back together. If additional chain length is needed, there may be a small material charge, but we'll always do our best to make it right.

Our 14k gold filled chains are generally safe for people with metal sensitivities because the thick gold layer prevents contact with the base metal. Solid gold is the best choice for the most sensitive skin. Sterling silver is 92.5% pure silver and is also generally well-tolerated.

If you have a known severe metal allergy, we recommend consulting with your doctor before getting permanent jewelry and discussing solid gold as your safest option.

Ear Piercings (Foley, AL Only)

Yes! We can pierce ears for children. We use a gentle hand-pressured system — not piercing guns — which is much quieter and more comfortable for kids. A parent or guardian must be present for minors and will need to provide consent.

Earlobe piercings typically heal in 6–8 weeks with proper aftercare. Cartilage piercings take longer — usually 3–9 months. We include an aftercare solution with every piercing and will provide you with full aftercare instructions at your appointment.

Yes. All of our starter jewelry is implant-grade and hypoallergenic, suitable for sensitive skin and new piercings. The starter jewelry and aftercare solution are included in the piercing price.

Piercing guns use blunt force to push jewelry through tissue, which causes more trauma, bruising, and a higher risk of complications. Our hand-pressured needle system is much more precise, causes less trauma to the tissue, heals faster, and is virtually silent — making it significantly more comfortable, especially for children.

Oyster Shucking Experience (Foley, AL Only)

It's simple and so much fun! You choose your oyster from our selection, then we guide you through shucking it open. Inside, you'll find your own natural pearl — every single one is unique in color, size, and shape. You can then choose to have us set your pearl into a necklace on the spot as a one-of-a-kind keepsake.

Yes! Every oyster in our selection is guaranteed to contain a real pearl. Pearl sizes, shapes, and colors vary naturally — that's what makes each experience so unique. You might find a classic white pearl, a lavender pearl, a peach pearl, or even a rare one with a beautiful luster.

The Pearl Reveal (shucking the oyster and discovering your pearl) is $30. If you'd like your pearl set into a necklace on the spot, the total is $55 (includes the Pearl Reveal + necklace setting).
