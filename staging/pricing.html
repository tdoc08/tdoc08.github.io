Solid Gold

All solid gold chains are priced at market rate and fluctuate with the current gold market. All karats available — 10k, 14k, and 18k.

Why market price? The cost of solid gold moves daily with commodity markets. We source our chains fresh to guarantee you're getting genuine solid gold at a fair price.

14k gold filled has a thick layer of real gold bonded to a base metal core. It's durable, tarnish-resistant, and safe for sensitive skin. Solid gold is pure gold all the way through — the highest quality available but priced at market rate. Both are beautiful; solid gold is the investment piece.

Yes — all listed prices include both the chain and the on-site welding service. There is no separate "labor" fee. What you see is what you pay.
