The Experience

How It Works

Three magical steps to your own one-of-a-kind pearl keepsake. No experience required — just curiosity and excitement.

Choose Your Oyster

Select from our curated oysters, each holding a natural pearl inside. The anticipation alone is half the fun. No two pearls are alike — yours could be white, cream, gold, or even pink.

Shuck & Reveal

With guidance from our team, you'll open your oyster and discover your pearl. This is the moment — the reveal is pure magic. We capture it for you so you can relive it forever.

Set It in a Necklace

Love your pearl? We set it in a beautiful sterling silver or gold-filled necklace right on the spot. Add it to your existing permanent jewelry — or make it permanent too.
