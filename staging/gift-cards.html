How It Works

Purchase Online

Buy any amount through our secure Square gift card store. A digital gift card is sent instantly to the recipient's email.

Book an Appointment

The recipient books their appointment at either our Foley, AL or West Palm Beach, FL location — or catches us at a popup event.

Choose & Redeem

At their appointment, they choose their perfect piece. The gift card is applied at checkout. Any remaining balance stays on the card for a future visit.

Gift Card FAQ
