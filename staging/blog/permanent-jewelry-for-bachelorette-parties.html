Permanent Jewelry for Bachelorette Parties

February 15, 2026  ·  By Evermore Permanent Jewelry

Matching bracelets for the whole squad. A hands-on experience everyone actually remembers. And a piece of jewelry the bride wears forever — literally. It's no surprise that permanent jewelry has become one of the most popular bachelorette party activities of 2025.

Here's why groups are choosing permanent jewelry over traditional bachelorette activities, and how to plan a private booking for your celebration.

Planning a bachelorette? We accommodate groups at both locations. For private bookings and exclusive studio access, submit a private event inquiry. We travel for destination events too — Gulf Shores, Destin, 30A, and beyond.

Why Permanent Jewelry Makes the Perfect Bachelorette Activity

It's an Experience, Not Just a Product

Everyone watches the micro-welder in action. There's something genuinely exciting about getting a piece of jewelry welded right onto your wrist — and for a group, it becomes a shared moment everyone talks about.

Matching Pieces for the Whole Group

Each person chooses their own chain style and metal, but the whole group can get matching or coordinated pieces. A shared bachelorette bracelet is a keepsake the bride and her crew will wear for years.

It Works for All Budgets in the Group

Pieces start at $85 (sterling silver) and $95 (gold filled). Group members can choose their own budget while still participating in the same experience.

We Travel to You

Planning a destination bachelorette? We're available for popup events in Gulf Shores, Orange Beach, Destin, 30A, and other Gulf Coast destinations. Inquire about travel availability when you submit your event request.

Add an Extra Wow Factor: Oyster Shucking & Ear Piercings

If your bachelorette is near Gulf Shores or the Alabama Gulf Coast, you're in luck. Our Foley, AL studio offers two experiences you can add to your permanent jewelry booking:

Oyster Shucking Experience ($30 + $25 necklace)

Each guest cracks open a real oyster and reveals a genuine pearl inside — then turns it into a necklace on the spot. It's a treasure hunt, a keepsake, and a story all in one. Bachelorette groups love it because every pearl is different — the bride might get a round white pearl, the maid of honor a gold baroque. No two experiences are the same.

Ear Piercings ($75)

Add a boutique ear piercing to your visit. We use a hand-pressured needle system — no piercing guns, no trauma, just a clean precise result in a luxury studio environment. Great for guests who want a longer-lasting memento than a bracelet they might remove someday.

How to Book a Group Bachelorette Experience

Option 1: Walk-In Group (Smaller Groups, Up to ~6)

For smaller groups, you can book individual appointments at the same time slot — no private event required. Just mention in your booking notes that you're a bachelorette group and we'll coordinate to have everyone's experience flow together. Book through our online scheduling and select the same time slot for each person.

Option 2: Private Booking (Groups of 6+, or for Exclusive Studio Access)

For larger groups or if you want the whole studio to yourselves, book a private event. We'll reserve the full studio for your group, set the mood, and make the whole experience feel exclusive. Travel options available for destination bachelorettes in Gulf Shores, Orange Beach, Destin, 30A, and beyond.

View private event details and book →

What to Expect on the Day

  1. Arrive together — we coordinate timing so everyone is done at roughly the same time
  2. Browse chains — each guest selects their chain style and metal from our collection
  3. Watch the weld — the micro-welding process takes about 5 minutes per person and is genuinely exciting to watch
  4. Photograph the moment — the "weld shot" is a must-have bachelorette photo
  5. Walk out together — everyone leaves wearing their new permanent piece

Pro tip for the bride's piece: Consider a charms add-on for the bride only — a birthstone, a small initial, or the date of the wedding. It turns a beautiful bracelet into a forever-meaningful heirloom.
