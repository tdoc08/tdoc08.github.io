Our Piercing System

Safe, Gentle, and FDA-Approved

No "Guns" Here

Unlike spring-loaded guns that "shoot" through the ear, our hand-pressured instrument is quiet, accurate, and gentle. It's so safe it's used in pediatrician offices.

100% Sterile

We use fully enclosed, single-use earring cartridges. This prevents cross-contamination and ensures that only the sterilized earring touches your ear.

Patented Safety Back

Our earrings feature a Safety Back™ that shields the sharp post and prevents the earring from being over-tightened, allowing for optimal air flow and faster healing.
