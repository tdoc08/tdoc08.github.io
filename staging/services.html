Permanent Jewelry

Our signature service. Each piece is custom-fitted and welded directly onto your wrist, ankle, neck, or finger using a small, painless spark welder. No clasp. No taking it off. Just beautiful, seamless jewelry that's truly yours forever.

We carry chains in 14k gold filled, sterling silver, and solid gold (market price). Charms, birthstones, and custom additions are available.

  • Clasp-free — no latch to fidget with or lose
  • Waterproof — safe for showers, pools, ocean
  • Airport-friendly — no need to remove at security
  • Custom-fitted to your wrist on the spot
  • Available in bracelets, anklets, necklaces, hand chains & rings

Boutique Ear Piercings

Skip the mall kiosks and treat yourself to a truly elevated piercing experience. We use a hand-pressured system — no loud guns, no blunt force. Just clean, precise, and quiet.

All jewelry is hypoallergenic. Starter jewelry and aftercare solution are included with every piercing. Perfect for lobe piercings on all ages, curated ear stacks, and more.

  • Hand-pressured system — no piercing guns
  • Sterile single-use cartridges
  • Hypoallergenic starter jewelry included
  • Aftercare solution included
  • Safe for children and all ages
  • Ideal for curated ear stacks

Oyster Shucking Experience

One of our most unique and memorable experiences. Choose your oyster, shuck it open, and discover the real pearl hiding inside. Then we'll turn your pearl into a beautiful keepsake necklace on the spot.

It's a perfect add-on to a permanent jewelry appointment, a fun group activity, or a one-of-a-kind gift experience. Every pearl is completely unique — just like you.

  • Choose your oyster from our selection
  • Shuck it open yourself (with guidance)
  • Discover your one-of-a-kind natural pearl
  • Pearl set into a necklace in-store
  • Perfect for groups, events & special occasions
